# Jobs To Be Done (JTBD)

## Primary Job
When I need something for my pet, I want to quickly buy a product I trust so that my pet stays healthy and safe.

## Functional Jobs
- Find pet products suitable for my specific pet
- Ensure product quality and authenticity
- Receive the product quickly without hassle

## Emotional Jobs
- Feel confident that I am making the right choice
- Avoid guilt or anxiety from buying the wrong product
- Feel like a responsible pet owner

## Social Jobs
- Be seen as caring and attentive towards my pet
- Avoid negative judgment from vets, friends, or family
